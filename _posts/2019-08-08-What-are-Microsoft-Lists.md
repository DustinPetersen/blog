What are Sharepoint lists? Sharepoint lists are essentially a database that used within SharePoint.

The data will be stored in Columns and rows similar to an Excel spreadsheet but due to it being within SharePoint you have access to all the SharePoint features such as Views, Data protection & the data can be shared easily.


The big change with Microsoft Lists as it is it's no directly avaible as its own app and can be used as an app within teams.
